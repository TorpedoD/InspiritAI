Questions: 
On Pentecost, a group of about 120 people were gathered together and the Holy Spirit descended. Why is it significant that it was the same day as Shavuot?


What is the Greek word for ‘church?’

What are 3 ways the Church spread and grew?


Answers:
Shavuot was when God’s law descended. On Pentecost, the Holy Spirit descended. The Law was written on stone tablets on Shavuot, but on Pentecost it was now written on the people’s hearts. 
Ekklesia
Message is proclaimed (people accept and get baptized), Persecution, and Evangelism (person to person).
