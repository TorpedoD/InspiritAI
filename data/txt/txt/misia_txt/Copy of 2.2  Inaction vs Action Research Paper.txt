Action vs Inaction Research Paper

Applying what we already have learned about being a good or remarkable citizen, the goal is to create a two-page double spaced research paper on the consequences of inaction and how they greatly outweigh the negative effects of taking action. 
You must use the following guidelines: 
Come up with three examples of action being taken by a small group or individual throughout history and how their impact has influenced changed our world today (6 marks)
Explain what the action was and how they went about it
State and recognize the type of issue that they were facing (3 marks)
Think and communicate the consequences of their potential inaction (3 marks)
Are people taking action about this today? (3 marks)
What were the consequences of their action? (3 marks) 

Communication and Writing Style (2 marks)

Total: 20 marks
